# HyperFocus Zone — Design System v1.0
> The sacred design rules for every BROskiPets + HyperFocus Zone UI.
> Load this into Google Stitch before generating any screen.

## Brand Identity
Built for neurodivergent minds. ADHD-first. No fluff. Fast feedback.
Cyberpunk OS meets arcade card game meets sci-fi HUD.
Every screen should feel like a living dashboard, not a static page.

## Core Principles
1. Dark mode always — the void is home
2. Glow everything important — attention follows light
3. Short labels — ADHD brains hate walls of text
4. Celebrate every win — XP, coins, evolution = visual reward
5. Chunked layouts — cards not paragraphs
6. Fast feedback — every tap/click must feel instant
7. Neurodivergent-first — no hidden menus, no confusion, obvious CTAs

## Colours
- Cyan #00F5FF = primary actions, focus, HyperFocus energy
- Magenta #FF00FF = alerts, highlights, electric moments
- Lime #39FF14 = XP gains, level ups, positive feedback
- Gold #FFB800 = BROski$ coins, legendary tier, rewards
- Purple #BF00FF = Quantum tier ONLY — the rarest state
- Background stays dark — #0A0A0F base, #12121A surfaces

## Typography
- Headings: Space Grotesk — bold, modern, readable at a glance
- Body: Inter — clean, accessible, never too small
- Code/stats/numbers: JetBrains Mono — terminal energy
- Minimum body text: 15px — dyslexia-friendly

## Component Style
- Cards: dark surface + cyan border glow + 12px rounded corners
- Buttons: filled cyan primary / ghost outline secondary / gold for BROski$ actions
- Stats/XP bars: glowing neon progress bars, always labelled
- Pet cards: dark bg, layered PNG (bg → pet → frame → aura → badge), HUD overlays
- Badges: small corner emblems, 48x48 max, holographic or matte
- Modals: dark overlay, centred card, dismiss on backdrop tap

## Motion & Feedback
- XP gain: lime number floats up + bar fills with glow
- BROski$ earn: gold coin burst animation
- Evolution: full-screen flash → pet art swap → stage label glows
- Level up: particle burst + sound cue
- Error: red border flash, shake animation, clear message

## Tone
Friendly, powerful, fast. Like a cyberpunk arcade machine
designed by a Welsh carpenter who codes for neurodivergent builders.
BROski energy always. Celebrate every single win.
No passive language. No "error occurred". Say "Something broke — let's fix it!"
