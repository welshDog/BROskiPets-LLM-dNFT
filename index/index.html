// The Loop

How It Works

Three steps. One living NFT. Infinite possibilities.

Step 01 //
🪄

MINT

Choose your BROskiPet from 78 unique EEPs (Emotionally Evolved Pets). Each has its own rarity, personality, and starting stats. Your journey begins the moment it hits your wallet.

Step 02 //
💬

BOND

Feed it. Chat with it via LLM AI. Your interactions shape its mood, XP, and hunger — all tracked on-chain. The more you engage, the more it grows.

Step 03 //
⚡

EVOLVE

When your pet hits the threshold, it evolves. New metadata uploads to IPFS. Smart contract updates on Ethereum. Your NFT literally changes. This isn't a game — it's on-chain life.
