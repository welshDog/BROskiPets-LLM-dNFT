## 🐾 What Is BROskiPets?

BROskiPets are **AI-powered dynamic NFTs** that:
- 🧠 Have a **real LLM brain** — chat with your pet, it remembers you
- 🎨 **Evolve their appearance** based on your dev activity (commits, missions, XP)
- ⛓️ Live **on-chain** (Sepolia testnet → Ethereum mainnet)
- 📦 Store metadata on **IPFS via Pinata** — permanent, decentralised
- 🎓 Graduate as **rare WelshDogEep** NFTs (only 3 ever mintable!)

This is the most ambitious part of the Hyper Merge ecosystem — and it needs your support to ship.
