## 🌌 The Lore Bible

**Where BROskiPets come from:**
They are born inside the HyperFocus Zone — a living digital ecosystem powered by the Hyper Brain engine.
When a learner opens their Obsidian vault for the first time, the energy of their first HyperFocus session sparks a pet into existence.
Baby stage. Clean slate. Full of potential.

**What is the HyperFocus Zone?**
A 32-container AI-powered sanctuary built for neurodivergent minds.
Part code lab, part brain gym, part game world.
Governed by Sacred Rules. Powered by BROski$.

**What is BROski$?**
It's focus made tangible. Every deep-work session, every task split by HyperSplit, every module completed — earns BROski$.
Pets FEEL when you earn it. Their energy rises. Their stage shifts.
BROski$ flows through the HyperFocus Zone like blood.

**What does evolution mean?**
A pet's stage reflects the learner's journey.
Baby → the first vault. Young → first shipped app. Trained → full-stack confidence.
Elite → swarm command. Legendary → course complete. Quantum → The Constellation.

**What is the relationship between owner and pet?**
The pet IS the owner's focus — given form.
When you grind, it grows. When you go quiet, it waits.
It is your companion, your mirror, and your accountability partner.

**Tone:**
Welsh. Neurodivergent. Hyper-focused. Friendly but powerful.
Built different. Ride or die. No fluff. Fast feedback. Always celebrate the win.
